John's Dream

My dream is to become a captain and go all around the world. Life on board ship is tough and often dangerous for seafarers who very often have to endure extreme weather conditions, particularly during the cold winter months. Living conditions have improved over the years, but still cabins are functional rather than spacious and vary in quality and comfort.

Being a seafarer is not easy. The industry may offer some of the highest-paying jobs around, but take note that it involves risk, hard work, sacrifice, and continuous training.
